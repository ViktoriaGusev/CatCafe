About Us

Me:

Hey my name is Vika and I'm the owner of the place.
I'm 28 years old, from Rishon Lezion.
I decided to open this Cat Cafe because I love cats and coffee!
I've been to a few Cat Cafes in europe and I loved the concept.
To be able to save some cats from the streets and let them live here,
feed them and let them be pet by so many people who are cat lovers!
Plus the costumers can enjoy their coffee while they have a purring cat on the lap.
So if you want to visit us you should meet our cats.
Here are some of them:




Ekler:

Ekler is only 10 months old and like all the kids - a bit crazy!
We found her in a garage of my old workplace.
She was only 3 months old and people fed her but not enough at all.
I instantly fell in love with her and decided to take her here.
She's so playful and sometimes you will get a bite just because she wanted to.
But despite her craziness she likes pets and purr and lay on your lap.
Try to play with her and you will enjoy your time!




Niki:

Niki is 3 years old and we found her in a trip to the Dead Sea.
I saw a 2 months old ginger kitten in the heat of Dead Sea area in the summer.
I just couldn't leave her there to suffer from the heat.
At first I tried to find her a new home.
But after a week I fell in love with her and couldn't let anyone take her.
Niki is VERY cute. Loves cuddles. Loves talking a lot!
But don't you dare to touch her if she doesn't want to be touched.
You can let her smell your hands and if you see a bit resistence - Don't touch!
Try talking to her, she will meow at you.




Lesia:

Lesia is our oldest, 4 years old.
We found her in a box near a dumpster and she was around 1-2 weeks old.
I was a bit scared to lose her because she was basically a newborn.
After a while she got bigger and stronger and she stayed with us.
She's very scared of... everything.
Better not move too much or do noises around her because she will be afraid.
She loves cuddles and pets and kisses but she doesn't like other cats.
Try to communicate with her, she might run away or love you lots.
